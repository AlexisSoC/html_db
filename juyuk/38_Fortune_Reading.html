Hexagram Interpretation

This hexagram is known as ䷥ (Hexagram 38 - Opposition). It symbolizes differences, conflict, and divergence. The hexagram suggests that this is a time of conflicting views or divergent paths. It advises being aware of differences and finding ways to navigate them without creating further conflict. The hexagram encourages understanding and accepting differences, while also looking for common ground and maintaining balance despite opposing views.
