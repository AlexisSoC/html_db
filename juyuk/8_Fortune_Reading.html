Hexagram Interpretation

This hexagram is known as ䷇ (Hexagram 8 - Holding Together). It symbolizes unity, support, and mutual cooperation. The hexagram suggests that collaboration and building connections with others will lead to success. It advises seeking guidance, building relationships, and uniting with like-minded individuals to achieve common goals. The hexagram encourages teamwork and a sense of shared purpose.

One Month: Potential for stable health. Maintain a sense of unity and support in your health practices.
