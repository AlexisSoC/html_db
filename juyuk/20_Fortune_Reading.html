Hexagram Interpretation

This hexagram is known as ䷓ (Hexagram 20 - Contemplation). It symbolizes reflection, observation, and gaining insight. The hexagram suggests that it is a time to step back, reflect, and observe situations with a calm and detached mindset. It advises focusing on understanding and gaining a clear perspective before taking any action. The hexagram encourages careful consideration, observation of others, and being mindful of the bigger picture.
