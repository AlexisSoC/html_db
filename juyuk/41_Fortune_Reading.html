Hexagram Interpretation

This hexagram is known as ䷧ (Hexagram 41 - Decrease). It symbolizes reduction, letting go, and simplifying. The hexagram suggests that this is a time to focus on reducing excess and unnecessary burdens in your life. It advises being willing to let go of what is no longer useful and making space for what truly matters. The hexagram encourages embracing simplicity and making conscious choices to declutter your life.
