Hexagram Interpretation

This hexagram is known as ䷏ (Hexagram 16 - Enthusiasm). It symbolizes joy, inspiration, and the power of positive energy. The hexagram suggests that enthusiasm and optimism can bring success and attract others to your cause. It advises taking initiative and acting with confidence, while also inspiring others to do the same. The hexagram encourages spreading positivity and taking action to create a vibrant and dynamic environment.
