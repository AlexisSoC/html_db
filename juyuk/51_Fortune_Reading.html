Hexagram Interpretation

This hexagram is known as ䷧ (Hexagram 51 - The Arousing). It symbolizes shock, movement, and the power of sudden change. The hexagram suggests that this is a time to be aware of the potential for sudden changes in your life. It advises remaining calm and grounded during unexpected events and embracing the opportunities that arise from them. The hexagram encourages you to harness the energy of change and use it to propel yourself forward.
