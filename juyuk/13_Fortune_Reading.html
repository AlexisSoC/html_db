Hexagram Interpretation

This hexagram is known as ䷌ (Hexagram 13 - Fellowship with Men). It symbolizes community, cooperation, and working together toward common goals. The hexagram suggests that success can be achieved through harmonious relationships and mutual support. It advises focusing on common interests, building trust, and fostering goodwill. This hexagram encourages collaboration and unity with others to achieve shared objectives.
