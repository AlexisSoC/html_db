Hexagram Interpretation

This hexagram is known as ䷧ (Hexagram 53 - Development). It symbolizes gradual progress, cultivation, and the importance of patience in achieving goals. The hexagram suggests that this is a time for steady development and long-term growth. It advises being patient and nurturing your efforts, allowing them to unfold naturally. The hexagram encourages focusing on the process of growth and valuing persistence over immediate results.
