Hexagram Interpretation

This hexagram is known as ䷍ (Hexagram 11 - Peace). It symbolizes harmony, prosperity, and balance. The hexagram suggests a time of tranquility and positive development. It advises being open and generous, as well as maintaining equilibrium in all areas of life. Peace and harmony can be achieved through cooperation and alignment with others. The hexagram encourages fostering goodwill and creating a supportive environment.
