Hexagram Interpretation

This hexagram is known as ䷗ (Hexagram 24 - Return). It symbolizes renewal, new beginnings, and the return to a more favorable time. The hexagram suggests that this is a time of fresh starts and rejuvenation. It advises returning to what is important and letting go of what is no longer serving you. The hexagram encourages embracing change, being open to new opportunities, and trusting in the natural cycle of life.
