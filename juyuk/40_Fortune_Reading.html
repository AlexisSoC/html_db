Hexagram Interpretation

This hexagram is known as ䷧ (Hexagram 40 - Deliverance). It symbolizes liberation, relief, and overcoming obstacles. The hexagram suggests that this is a time for releasing burdens and moving forward after difficulties. It advises letting go of what no longer serves you and embracing freedom. The hexagram encourages taking positive actions to improve your situation and to help others find their own relief as well.

One Month: Potential for good health if you prioritize your well-being and let go of unhealthy habits.
