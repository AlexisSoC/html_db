Hexagram Interpretation

This hexagram is known as ䷥ (Hexagram 45 - Gathering Together). It symbolizes unity, community, and cooperation. The hexagram suggests that this is a time for coming together and building strong connections. It advises embracing collaboration and seeking support from others. The hexagram encourages forming alliances and focusing on shared goals to achieve collective success.
