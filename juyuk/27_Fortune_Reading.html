Hexagram Interpretation

This hexagram is known as ䷚ (Hexagram 27 - Nourishment). It symbolizes nourishment, nurturing, and taking care of what is essential. The hexagram suggests that this is a time to focus on both physical and spiritual nourishment. It advises paying attention to what you are feeding yourself, not just in terms of food, but also in terms of thoughts, emotions, and activities. The hexagram encourages being mindful of what sustains you and letting go of what does not contribute to your well-being.

One Month: Potential for good health if you consistently prioritize nourishment and self-care.
