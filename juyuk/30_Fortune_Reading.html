Hexagram Interpretation

This hexagram is known as ䷝ (Hexagram 30 - Clarity). It symbolizes light, illumination, and clear insight. The hexagram suggests that this is a time to gain clarity and understanding in all areas of life. It advises being open to new insights, seeking knowledge, and avoiding confusion or obscurity. The hexagram encourages using the power of clarity to illuminate the path ahead, making informed decisions, and seeing things as they truly are.
