Hexagram Interpretation

This hexagram is known as ䷦ (Hexagram 39 - Obstruction). It symbolizes obstacles, difficulties, and facing challenges. The hexagram suggests that this is a time to confront and overcome difficulties. It advises being patient and careful in dealing with obstacles, and not giving up in the face of adversity. The hexagram encourages perseverance and finding ways to move around or overcome challenges, rather than forcing through them directly.
