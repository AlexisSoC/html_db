Hexagram Interpretation

This hexagram is known as ䷆ (Hexagram 15 - Modesty). It symbolizes humility, self-restraint, and being mindful of one’s actions. The hexagram suggests that success can be achieved through modesty and maintaining a balanced, humble approach. It advises avoiding arrogance and excess, as true power lies in self-awareness and a calm demeanor. Modesty allows for progress without attracting unwanted attention or creating conflicts.
