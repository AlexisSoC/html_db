Hexagram Interpretation

This hexagram is known as ䷅ (Hexagram 6 - Conflict). It symbolizes a period of tension, disagreements, and challenges in communication. This hexagram advises caution, careful planning, and clarity of thought when dealing with disputes. The hexagram suggests that not every conflict needs to be resolved immediately; sometimes it is better to wait for the right moment to address the situation. Avoid confrontations and focus on understanding the root cause of issues.
