Hexagram Interpretation

This hexagram is known as ䷐ (Hexagram 17 - Following). It symbolizes adaptability, acceptance, and harmonious alignment with one’s circumstances. The hexagram suggests that it is a time to go with the flow and be open to change. Rather than trying to assert control, allow events to unfold naturally. Success comes through being flexible, understanding others, and finding common ground. The hexagram advises patience and a willingness to follow rather than lead.

One Month: Potential for stable health if you remain adaptable and open to making changes as needed.
