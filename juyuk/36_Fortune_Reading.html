Hexagram Interpretation

This hexagram is known as ䷣ (Hexagram 36 - Darkening of the Light). It symbolizes concealment, obscurity, and protecting oneself from harm. The hexagram suggests that this is a time to hide your true intentions and avoid drawing attention to yourself. It advises being cautious and protecting yourself from negative influences or dangers. The hexagram encourages maintaining a low profile and waiting for the right time to reveal your full potential.
