Hexagram Interpretation

This hexagram is known as ䷙ (Hexagram 26 - The Taming Power of the Great). It symbolizes strength, control, and the power to overcome challenges through restraint and careful planning. The hexagram suggests that success can be achieved through perseverance and self-discipline. It advises using strength wisely and focusing on long-term goals rather than immediate gratification. The hexagram encourages maintaining inner strength and being prepared to take action when the time is right.
