Hexagram Interpretation

This hexagram is known as ䷧ (Hexagram 25 - Innocence). It symbolizes naturalness, spontaneity, and being in harmony with the natural flow of life. The hexagram encourages acting without ulterior motives and approaching situations with sincerity and integrity. It suggests that the best course of action is to remain pure-hearted and avoid overthinking or planning too far ahead.
