Hexagram Interpretation

This hexagram is known as ䷁ (Kun), representing Earth. It symbolizes receptivity, nurturing, and the yielding nature of the Earth. It is a sign of support, patience, and being open to external influences without imposing one's own will. The hexagram suggests a need to remain passive, adaptable, and receptive to change rather than taking assertive actions.
