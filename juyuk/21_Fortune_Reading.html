Hexagram Interpretation

This hexagram is known as ䷔ (Hexagram 21 - Biting Through). It symbolizes resolving obstacles, eliminating corruption, and taking decisive action. The hexagram suggests that it is a time to address problems directly and remove obstacles that hinder progress. It advises firmness and clarity in confronting issues, whether in personal relationships, business, or other areas of life. The hexagram encourages taking a strong and resolute approach to overcome difficulties.
