Hexagram Interpretation

This hexagram is known as ䷧ (Hexagram 49 - Revolution). It symbolizes change, transformation, and the process of renewal. The hexagram suggests that this is a time for significant change and a re-evaluation of the status quo. It advises embracing transformation and being open to new ideas and ways of thinking. The hexagram encourages you to take action towards change and to participate actively in the process of renewal.
