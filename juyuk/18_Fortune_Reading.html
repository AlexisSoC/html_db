Hexagram Interpretation

This hexagram is known as ䷓ (Hexagram 18 - Work on What Has Been Spoiled). It symbolizes correction, repair, and resolving past issues. The hexagram suggests that this is a time to address mistakes, fix problems, and restore balance. It advises taking a thoughtful and deliberate approach to resolving issues, whether in personal relationships, business, or other areas of life. The hexagram encourages perseverance and the willingness to make necessary changes to achieve long-term success.
