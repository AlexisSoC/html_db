Hexagram Interpretation

This hexagram is known as ䷧ (Hexagram 43 - Breakthrough). It symbolizes decisiveness, clarity, and the removal of obstacles. The hexagram suggests that this is a time to take decisive action and confront challenges head-on. It advises being clear about your intentions and moving forward with confidence. The hexagram encourages you to embrace the opportunity for change and to break free from limitations that may have held you back.
