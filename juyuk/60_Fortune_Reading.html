Hexagram Interpretation

This hexagram is known as ䷧ (Hexagram 60 - Limitation). It symbolizes boundaries, constraints, and the need for discipline. The hexagram suggests that this is a time to establish limits and to focus on moderation. It advises being mindful of your resources and setting boundaries to maintain balance in your life. The hexagram encourages you to embrace limitations as a means of fostering growth and development.
