Hexagram Interpretation

This hexagram is known as ䷍ (Hexagram 14 - Great Possession). It symbolizes abundance, success, and prosperity. The hexagram suggests that great achievements and wealth can be attained, but it also advises being humble and using resources wisely. It is a time of abundance, and the hexagram encourages generosity, sharing, and acting with integrity. Success should be used to benefit others and create positive outcomes.
