Hexagram Interpretation

This hexagram is known as ䷈ (Hexagram 9 - Small Taming). It symbolizes the power of gentle restraint and the importance of small, consistent efforts. The hexagram suggests that while large actions may not be successful at this time, small and steady steps can lead to progress. It advises patience, subtle influence, and careful planning. Focus on details and gradual improvement rather than big changes.
