Hexagram Interpretation

This hexagram is known as ䷉ (Hexagram 10 - Treading). It symbolizes caution, careful conduct, and attentiveness to one’s actions. The hexagram suggests that while progress is possible, it is essential to tread carefully and avoid risky behavior. It advises remaining calm, respectful, and mindful of potential dangers. Success can be achieved through careful planning and awareness of one’s surroundings.

One Week: Health may fluctuate. Stay vigilant and take preventative measures if needed.

One Month: Gradual improvement in health is possible if caution and self-care are maintained.

One Month: Business growth is possible if risks are minimized and actions are well thought out.
