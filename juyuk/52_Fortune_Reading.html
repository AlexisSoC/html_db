Hexagram Interpretation

This hexagram is known as ䷧ (Hexagram 52 - Keeping Still). It symbolizes stillness, meditation, and the importance of inner reflection. The hexagram suggests that this is a time to embrace stillness and seek clarity within yourself. It advises taking a step back to reflect on your thoughts and feelings. The hexagram encourages you to find peace in silence and to cultivate a strong inner foundation.
