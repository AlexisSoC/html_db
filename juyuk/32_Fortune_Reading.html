Hexagram Interpretation

This hexagram is known as ䷟ (Hexagram 32 - Duration). It symbolizes endurance, perseverance, and long-term stability. The hexagram suggests that this is a time to focus on maintaining steady progress and not giving up. It advises staying committed to your goals and being patient, as success will come through consistent effort over time. The hexagram encourages perseverance and staying true to one’s principles, even when faced with difficulties.
