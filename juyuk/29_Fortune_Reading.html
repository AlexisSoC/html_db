Hexagram Interpretation

This hexagram is known as ䷜ (Hexagram 29 - The Abysmal). It symbolizes danger, uncertainty, and facing challenges. The hexagram suggests that this is a time to be cautious and careful in all endeavors. It advises being aware of potential pitfalls and navigating carefully to avoid getting caught in difficulties. The hexagram encourages patience, perseverance, and a clear mind to overcome obstacles and safely reach the other side of any challenge.
