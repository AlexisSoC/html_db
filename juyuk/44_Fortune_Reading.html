Hexagram Interpretation

This hexagram is known as ䷥ (Hexagram 44 - Coming to Meet). It symbolizes the encounter of potential and opportunity. The hexagram suggests that this is a time to be aware of the influence of others and to be prepared for new encounters. It advises caution and discernment in interactions, as not all opportunities may be beneficial. The hexagram encourages openness to new experiences while also being mindful of the potential consequences of such encounters.
