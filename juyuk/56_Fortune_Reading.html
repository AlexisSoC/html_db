Hexagram Interpretation

This hexagram is known as ䷧ (Hexagram 56 - The Wanderer). It symbolizes travel, exploration, and the experience of being a guest. The hexagram suggests that this is a time to embrace new experiences and to be open to change. It advises maintaining adaptability and humility as you navigate unfamiliar circumstances. The hexagram encourages you to seek wisdom in new environments and to learn from the journeys you undertake.
