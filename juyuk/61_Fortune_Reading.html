Hexagram Interpretation

This hexagram is known as ䷧ (Hexagram 61 - Inner Truth). It symbolizes sincerity, communication, and the importance of genuine connections. The hexagram suggests that this is a time to focus on understanding your inner feelings and expressing them openly. It advises being authentic in your interactions and fostering trust in your relationships. The hexagram encourages you to seek truth and transparency in all aspects of your life.
