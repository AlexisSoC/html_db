Hexagram Interpretation

This hexagram is known as ䷆ (Hexagram 7 - The Army). It symbolizes discipline, organization, and strategic planning. The hexagram suggests that to achieve success, one must be prepared, organized, and willing to lead or follow as necessary. It advises building a strong foundation, setting clear goals, and maintaining discipline in all endeavors. Challenges may arise, but with proper strategy and teamwork, they can be overcome.
