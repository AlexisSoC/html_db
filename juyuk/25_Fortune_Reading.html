Hexagram Interpretation

This hexagram is known as ䷘ (Hexagram 25 - Innocence). It symbolizes simplicity, naturalness, and being true to oneself. The hexagram suggests that this is a time to focus on honesty, sincerity, and avoiding complications. It advises embracing simplicity and being open and genuine in all interactions. The hexagram encourages acting with a pure heart and trusting in the natural flow of events rather than trying to manipulate or control situations.
