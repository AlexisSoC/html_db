Hexagram Interpretation

This hexagram is known as ䷧ (Hexagram 55 - Abundance). It symbolizes plenty, prosperity, and the fulfillment of desires. The hexagram suggests that this is a time for enjoying the fruits of your labor and embracing abundance in your life. It advises sharing your wealth and resources with others, as generosity can lead to further blessings. The hexagram encourages you to appreciate what you have and to create an environment of abundance and joy.
