Hexagram Interpretation

This hexagram is known as ䷧ (Hexagram 46 - Pushing Upward). It symbolizes growth, advancement, and the process of climbing towards success. The hexagram suggests that this is a time to focus on steady progress and making consistent efforts. It advises embracing opportunities for growth while being patient and determined. The hexagram encourages working diligently towards your goals and supporting others in their ascent as well.
