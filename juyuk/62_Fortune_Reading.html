Hexagram Interpretation

This hexagram is known as ䷧ (Hexagram 62 - Preponderance of the Small). It symbolizes caution, attention to detail, and the importance of humility. The hexagram suggests that this is a time to focus on the small things and to exercise patience in your endeavors. It advises being mindful of your actions and maintaining a humble approach, as small efforts can lead to significant outcomes. The hexagram encourages you to pay attention to the details and to avoid overreaching.
