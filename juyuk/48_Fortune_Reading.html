Hexagram Interpretation

This hexagram is known as ䷧ (Hexagram 48 - The Well). It symbolizes nourishment, resources, and the importance of a solid foundation. The hexagram suggests that this is a time to focus on drawing from your inner resources and nurturing your well-being. It advises being mindful of what nourishes you, whether physically, emotionally, or spiritually. The hexagram encourages maintaining a strong foundation to support growth and renewal.

One Week: Health may improve through mindful choices and nurturing practices.

One Month: Potential for good health if you consistently draw from your inner resources and prioritize self-care.
